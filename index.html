About the Institute

River Research & Geo-Spatial Foundation (RRGSF) is dedicated to advancing river science and innovative geo-spatial solutions. Located in Dhupguri, Jalpaiguri, the institute focuses on Himalayan river morphodynamics, floodplain evolution, participatory GIS, and environmental sustainability. We collaborate with universities and research organizations through MoUs, training workshops, and community engagement programs.
