About Us

River Research & Geo-Spatial Foundation (RRGSF) is dedicated to advancing river research and providing innovative geo-spatial solutions. Located in Dhupguri, Jalpaiguri, West Bengal, RRGSF collaborates with higher education institutes through MoUs and organizes workshops, research projects, and analytical studies to support sustainable river and environmental management.

Our Services

River Research

Comprehensive research and analysis on river systems, hydrology, and fluvial processes.

Geo-Spatial Solutions

GIS mapping, spatial data analysis, remote sensing, and cartographic services.

Statistical Analysis

Data-driven insights and statistical modeling for environmental and geographical studies.

Workshops

Capacity-building workshops and training sessions on river science and geospatial technologies.

Academic Collaboration

Collaborative research and Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with higher education institutes.
