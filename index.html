About the Founder

Dr. Debarshi Ghosh
Assistant Professor in Geography, Dhupguri Girls' College
Geomorphologist | River Researcher | Geo-Spatial Modeler

Dr. Ghosh holds a Ph.D. from the University of North Bengal on “Fluvial Dynamics of the Chel River Basin.” He specializes in river morphology, floodplain processes, and participatory GIS applications in environmental research. His work bridges field-based hydrological observation with advanced remote sensing and geospatial modelling.

Research Team

Research Areas

  • Channel Dynamics & Morphometric Analysis of Himalayan Rivers
  • Floodplain Processes and Bank Erosion Monitoring
  • Participatory GIS and Community-Based Flood Resilience
  • RS-GIS Applications in Environmental Assessment
  • Geo-Statistical and Hydrological Modeling

Major Projects

  • DSTBT Project: Channel migration and land loss in the Ganga, Malda District (Ongoing).
  • MoES Project: Teesta River Channel Shifting in the Dooars Region after the 2023 GLOF.
