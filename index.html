About The Foundation

RRGSF is a premier research body in Dhupguri dedicated to the scientific study of rivers in the Himalayan foreland. We combine traditional field geomorphology with modern satellite remote sensing.

Our Operational Wings

Technical Wing

GIS/RS based Mapping and Problem Assessment

Focusing on satellite data analysis, hazard mapping, and technical modelling of river systems.

Social Wing

Social Water Management Programme

Engaging communities in water conservation, literacy campaigns, and participatory resource management.
